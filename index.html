Data Notes & Methodology
Important information about this analysis

Data Scope

This analysis includes all 22,606 tasks from the CIT_Tasks.xlsx file:

  • Planned tasks: 20,087 tasks (88.9%) — Scheduled collections based on route planning
  • Ad Hoc tasks: 2,519 tasks (11.1%) — Unscheduled collections added during operations
  • Analysis period: January 5–11, 2026 (7 days)

Task Status Definitions

Tasks are categorized into three completion statuses:

  • Done: Task completed successfully, bin collected (20,600 tasks / 91.1%)
  • Visited: Service point was visited but collection was not completed (598 tasks / 2.6%) — may indicate access issues, bin not ready, or other operational problems
  • To Do: Task was scheduled but service point was never visited (1,408 tasks / 6.2%) — indicates route coverage gaps

Operational Areas

The analysis covers 7 distinct operational areas with 33 active routes:

  • Zone 1: 5 routes — Day, Night-1, Night-2, Day (Friday), Night-1 (Friday)
  • Zone 2: 16 routes — Sub-zones A, B, C with Day/Night/Friday/Residence shifts
  • Zone 3: 4 routes — AlQan, Zyath Village, Zyath & Highway, Haqal & Jadeed
  • Zone 4: 4 routes — Nima, Bajdah, Bajdah (Friday), AlSuru
  • Zone 5: 3 routes — Shigri Village Left/Right, Shigri (Friday)
  • Zone 6: 1 route — Village B
  • Sharma-ALD: 1 route — Independent area not part of numbered zones

Route Naming Conventions

Some routes have minor naming differences between the Static Tour file and Tasks file. These are treated as equivalent:

  • "Sharma - ALD - Aldrees" (Static Tour) = "Sharma-ALD-Aldrees" (Tasks)
  • "Zone 4 - Nima - V 2.0" (Static Tour) = "Zone 4 - Nima V 2.0" (Tasks)
  • "Zone 5 (Shigri Village) - V 2.0" splits into Left/Right routes in Tasks
  • Some Friday routes vary in "(Friday)" vs "Friday" formatting

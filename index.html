Understanding This Analysis
How to interpret service point failure data

Repeat Offender Identification

Service points ranked by total incomplete tasks. Multiple appearances indicate:

  • Chronic access issues: Location may be difficult to reach
  • Bin problems: Container may be damaged, blocked, or overfilled
  • Route inefficiency: Poorly positioned in route sequence
  • Scheduling conflicts: Collection timing may not match availability

Status Interpretation

The failure status provides diagnostic information:

  • "To Do" failures: Vehicle never reached the point ‚Äî route/time issues
  • "Visited" failures: Vehicle arrived but couldn't collect ‚Äî physical barriers
  • Mixed status: Inconsistent issues ‚Äî needs field investigation

Days Failed Analysis

Service points failing on multiple days show different patterns:

  • 7/7 days: Systemic issue requiring immediate attention
  • 5-6 days: Frequent problem, likely structural cause
  • 2-4 days: Intermittent issue, may be circumstantial
  • 1 day: Isolated incident, lower priority

Recommended Actions

Based on this analysis, consider:

  • Conduct field visits to top 20 problem service points
  • Review route sequencing for high "To Do" service points
  • Check bin conditions for high "Visited" service points
  • Consider schedule adjustments for consistent failures
